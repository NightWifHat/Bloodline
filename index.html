Our Story

Born From
The Needle

Bloodline Inkwear was built by tattoo artists, for those who wear their identity proudly. We believe the most powerful art doesn't stay on walls — it moves through the world on people who refuse to be ordinary.

Every design in our collection originates from real tattoo flash — hand-drawn, hand-inked, then translated to premium garments that carry that same raw energy.

Malcolm Hilton

Founder & Lead Artist

Malcolm Hilton is the creative force behind every Bloodline design. A self-taught tattoo artist with roots in American Traditional and Neo-Traditional flash, Malcolm has spent years perfecting the kind of bold, timeless artwork that earns its place on skin — and now, on the clothes you wear every day. Every thread in the Bloodline collection carries his hand.
